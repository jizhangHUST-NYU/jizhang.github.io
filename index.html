About

I am an assistant chief scientist, expert engineer and architect in Huawei. I achieved my Ph.D. degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology in China. I have been a visting scholar at New York university and a postdoc in the university of Amsterdam. I have been an internship at Tencent Inc. as part of the Intelligent Cloud Storage Joint Research center of HUST. My research focuses on the architecture design and performance optimization for data management and storage systems. I had published papers in international conferences and journals including ACM SIGMOD, USENIX ATC, DAC, FAST, ICPP, VLDB, TPDS and VLDBJ, etc.
